Are ants invading your Encinitas home? Our professional ant control services eliminate Argentine ants, odorous house ants, carpenter ants, and other species that commonly infest coastal California properties. We provide same-day service and long-lasting results.

Common Ant Species in Encinitas

Encinitas homes commonly face infestations from Argentine ants, which are particularly aggressive, as well as odorous house ants that emit a rotten smell when crushed. Carpenter ants can cause structural damage by tunneling through wood. Our technicians are trained to identify and treat all ant species found in the Encinitas area.
