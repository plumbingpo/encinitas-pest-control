Bed Bug Treatment in Encinitas, California

Bed bugs are one of the most difficult pests to eliminate. Our professional bed bug treatment in Encinitas uses proven heat and chemical treatments to completely eradicate infestations. We serve homes, hotels, and rental properties throughout the area.

Our Bed Bug Treatment Process

  • Inspection: Thorough search for bed bugs and their harborages
  • Heat Treatment: Professional-grade heating equipment raises room temperature to lethal levels
  • Chemical Treatment: Targeted application of residual insecticides
  • Encasements: Protective covers for mattresses and box springs
  • Follow-up: Return visits to ensure complete elimination

Signs of Bed Bugs

  • Red, itchy bites on skin
  • Small blood spots on bedding
  • Dark spots (feces) on mattresses or sheets
  • Live bugs or shed skins
  • Musty odor in severe infestations

Why Professional Treatment is Necessary

Bed bugs have developed resistance to many over-the-counter treatments. Professional heat treatment is the most effective method, raising room temperatures to 120°F+ to kill all life stages. DIY attempts often fail and allow infestations to spread.
