Flea Control in Encinitas, California

Fleas can quickly infest your home through pets or wildlife. Our professional flea control services eliminate fleas at all life stages and help prevent re-infestation. We treat homes with pets and properties with historical flea problems.

Our Flea Treatment Process

  • Inspection: Identify flea hotspots and sources
  • Interior Treatment: Spray and dust treatments throughout the home
  • Pet Treatment Guidance: Recommend pet-safe solutions
  • Yard Treatment: Exterior applications for outdoor infestations
  • Follow-up: Schedule return visits to ensure complete control

Why Flea Control is Important

Fleas reproduce rapidly—a single female can lay 50 eggs per day. Flea bites cause itchy welts on humans and animals, and fleas can transmit tapeworms and other diseases. Professional treatment is essential for severe infestations.
