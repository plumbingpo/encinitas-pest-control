Mosquito Control in Encinitas, California

Mosquitoes aren't just annoying—they can transmit diseases like West Nile virus and Zika. Our mosquito control services in Encinitas reduce mosquito populations around your property for safer outdoor enjoyment.

Our Mosquito Control Process

  • Inspection: Identify breeding sites on your property
  • Source Reduction: Eliminate standing water sources
  • Larviciding: Treat water sources to kill mosquito larvae
  • Adulticiding: Fogging and residual treatments
  • Ongoing Maintenance: Regular service to maintain results

Mosquito Prevention Tips

  • Remove standing water from gutters, buckets, and containers
  • Keep pools and spas properly maintained
  • Trim vegetation and overgrown areas
  • Consider mosquito fish for ponds
