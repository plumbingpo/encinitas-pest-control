Rodent Control in Encinitas, California

Mice and rats pose serious health risks and can cause significant property damage. Our Encinitas rodent control services provide humane removal and exclusion to keep your property rodent-free. We serve homes and businesses throughout the Encinitas area.

Our Rodent Control Services

  • Inspection: Complete assessment of rodent activity and entry points
  • Trapping: Strategic placement of traps to capture rodents
  • Exclusion: Sealing of entry points to prevent future infestations
  • Sanitation: Cleanup of droppings and nesting materials
  • Prevention: Ongoing monitoring and maintenance programs

Signs of Rodent Infestation

  • Droppings in cabinets, drawers, or along walls
  • Gnaw marks on food packaging or wires
  • Nesting materials in hidden areas
  • Scratching sounds in walls or ceilings
  • Musky odor

Why Rodent Control Matters

Rodents can transmit diseases through their droppings, urine, and saliva. They can also contaminate food supplies and cause fires by chewing through electrical wires. Professional rodent control is essential for protecting your family's health and your property.
