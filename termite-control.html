Termite Control in Encinitas, California

Termites cause billions of dollars in damage annually across the United States. In Encinitas, California, subterranean termites and drywood termites pose serious threats to homes and businesses. Our comprehensive termite control services protect your property investment with proven treatment methods.

Our Termite Control Solutions

  • Termite Inspection: Thorough assessment of your property for signs of termite activity
  • Soil Treatment: Liquid termiticides applied around the foundation to create a protective barrier
  • Wood Treatment: Direct treatment of infested wood areas
  • Bait Systems: Termite baits that eliminate colonies over time
  • Repair Assessment: Evaluation of damage and referral to trusted repair contractors

Signs of Termite Infestation

  • Mud tubes on foundation walls or wooden structures
  • Discarded wings near windows or doors
  • Wood that sounds hollow when tapped
  • Small holes in drywall or wood
  • Frass (termite droppings) near wooden areas

Why Professional Termite Control is Essential

Termites work silently and continuously, often causing extensive damage before they're discovered. A professional inspection and treatment plan can save you thousands of dollars in repair costs. Our Encinitas termite control experts have the experience and equipment to identify and eliminate termite colonies before they cause major structural damage.
