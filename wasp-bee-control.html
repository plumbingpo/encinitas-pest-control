Wasp & Bee Control in Encinitas, California

Wasps and bees pose serious sting risks, especially for those with allergies. Our Encinitas pest control experts safely remove nests and colonies while protecting beneficial pollinators whenever possible.

Our Wasp & Bee Services

  • Nest Removal: Complete removal of established nests
  • Colony Extraction: Live removal of honey bee colonies
  • Trap Installation: Preventative traps for problem areas
  • Prevention: Seal entry points and nesting sites
  • Emergency Service: Same-day response for dangerous situations

Common Stinging Insects in Encinitas

Yellow jackets, paper wasps, and Africanized honey bees are common in our area. We identify the species and use appropriate removal methods. We also work with local beekeepers for live honey bee relocation when possible.
